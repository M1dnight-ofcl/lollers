I originally started lollers as a joke to put in my virtual timecapsule on the library computer. my plan was
to have it there for someone to find year down on the line. it was intended to be filled with art, memes,
logs, and my early works in VBScript. I made lollers as one ofn the viruses for it, and it turned out to
be much more. i am still working on it and i am also generally new to VBS, so dont expect it to be perfect.
i wanted to push VBS to its limits to show just how powerful it was for having basic in the name.
